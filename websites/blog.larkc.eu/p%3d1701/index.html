LarKC Early Adopters Tutorial at ISWC 2009

(by Mick Kerrigan, STI Innsbruck)

Following the highly successful early adopters tutorial at the 6th European Semantic Web Conference (ESWC) in June, we are organizing a second tutorial at the 8th International Semantic Web Conference (ISWC) on the 25th of October in Washington DC. The agenda of the tutorial has been slightly refactored based on the great feedback we had from the participants of the last event. The tutorial offers a great opportunity for participants to get access to early research results and technologies from the LarKC project. Also, as this years ISWC is located in the US, this event provides an opportunity for a wider audience outside europe to participate. More information on the tutorial can be found here. Looking forward to seeing you at ISWC!
