Excellent report on the state of the Semantic technologies market place

(by Frank)

 

Many (most? all?) consultant’s reports on “the state of industry X” are a heap of bla, and even worse when X=”semantic technologies”,but the recent report from David Provost, at http://semanticbusiness.blogspot.com/2008/09/report-on-cusp-global-review-of.html is a very good review of the current state of Semantic technologies indusry. 

It gives an insightful analysis, and profiles of the major vendors (and he made a  good choice of  vendors).

Even just the first 10 pages of executive summary are worth a read if you don’t have time to read all the full profiles.
