Technology Cooperation between Korea and EU

Seminar 2008 on Technology Cooperation between Korea and EU has been held successfully with more than 200 people from government, industry and research area at 28th August 2008 in Seoul, Korea.  The invited speaker includes Mr. Ignacio Asenjo at European Commission and more.  Saltlux’s CEO, Tony Lee has presented the audience EU FP participation experience of LarKC. This event was organized by Korean government that is seeking for ways to participate EU FP from 2014.
