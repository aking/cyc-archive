Real Time Cities

Image via Wikipedia

It seems that many cities may be starting to generate data suitable for LarKC scale processing. This autumn, San Francisco is apparently installing sensors on about a quarter of its parking spaces, with the aim of maintaining 85% occupation and dropping driving by about 30%. As someone who has often tried to find a parking space in S.F. this seems like a very good idea.
