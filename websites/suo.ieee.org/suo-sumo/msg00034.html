Allan,
   I'm confused about this as well.  I don't recall asking anyone to add
the GNU license to SUMO.  It may have been a misunderstanding since I
wanted to make sure that licenses were consistent on all the other products
we were releasing, like MILO and the DAML search engine.  Maybe Ian or
someone else thought that included SUMO.  I'm also puzzled because I
thought I downloaded the latest public SUMO just after I left Tek, to make
sure I had the right version.  What I downloaded does not have a GNU header.
   As I recall, the issue with some of our partners was not with SUMO being
GNU (since it wasn't), but with our domain ontologies having that license.
