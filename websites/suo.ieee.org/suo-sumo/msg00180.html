ATTENTION CUSTOMERS:(HOODIA SCAM)

 Please check the labels of "pure" hoodia products before buying.
Almost all "pure" hoodia products are padded with
fillers (up to 40%).

Be on the lookout for fillers such as cellulose, silica,
phosphates, magnesium and stearic acid appearing as
"Other ingredients". To be sure it's 100% pure,
look for a label that says, Other Ingredients: "None".

Please educate yourself before buying any hoodia product.
Be wary of vendors posing as consumer advocates with
"Hoodia Ranking Reports". These phony reports and their
"Top Picks" have been secretly created by vendors trying
to sell their own hyped-up, watered-down hoodia product.
