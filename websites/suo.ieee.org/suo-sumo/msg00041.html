Jim,
   My preference is simply not to include this license.  It was not present
on prior versions.  What is the reason, if any, that mandates its inclusion
now?  I suppose we could include the copyright text and just delete the
section below "Licensing for SUMO".  That would make the copyright clear,
and also not put any restrictions on the use of SUMO, other than claims of
conformance.

Adam
