Dear Adam,

I can tell you how things are in ISO TC184/SC4.

Here there are generally 3 layers:

The sub-committee website:
 - includes all the deliverables of the sub-committee
   - standards, white papers etc
 - minutes of meetings
 - announcements

Consortium websites talk about
 - consortium objectives
 - their projects to deliver deliverables for SC4
 - draft deliverables
 - consortium activities (promotional, conferences etc)

Company websites
 - promote the company
 - talk about relationship to standards activities
 - talk about how this company's products conform to the standards.
