Just as a list check, and following my  last note to this list about 
value based governance as a possible driver   for a world-class KR
standard, I like to communicate that I have  recently discovered that
that basic idea appears to be not precisely new.

In fact some (quite controversial) discussion of  "automata" and
"Commonwealth of States" can be find in Thomas Hobbes' 1651 introduction
to "The Leviathan" (Hobbes' name for Civitas -  Civilization), Oxford
World's Classic, Oxford University Press, N.Y 1998.  

The problem appears  to be how to get a  similar modern hybrid (human
and machine) system, a  complex and democratic "Leviathan", up and
running.
