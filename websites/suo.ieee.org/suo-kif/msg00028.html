The W3C working group has just published a new working draft that
makes recommendations on the treatment of Unicode characters in
WWW data.  It has a good summary of the issues with pointers to
many related documents on the web:

   http://www.w3.org/TR/charmod/

I recommend that we consider this document while we are
working on the KIF and CG standards.

John Sowa
