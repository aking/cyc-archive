Folks,
   Here's a list of basic changes to the SUO-KIF document that came out of 
our meeting this week.  The revised document is posted at 
http://ltsc.ieee.org/suo/suo-kif.html


*** section 4.2 ***

1.  Rename "alpha" as "special"

2.  Add the underscore and "special" to the definition for "wordchar"

3.  Add the sentence "Use of characters in "special" for word characters is 
discouraged as they may be given particular meaning in future versions of 
the standard or its extensions."


*** section 4.3 ***

4.  Rename "constant" to "word"


*** section 4.4 ***

5.  In our meeting I believe it was felt that just allowing special 
characters in words was enough to support inclusion of numbers.  The 
justification for this was in part that the BNF for numbers might be 
extensive and that therefore we could avoid a full definition but just 
including special characters.  However, I found a BNF definition for 
numbers in Java which is quite simple.  I hope this will be considered 
reasonable.  Add definition for "number" and "exponent" as follows:

number ::= [-] digit+ [. digit+] [exponent]
exponent ::= e [-] digit+

6.  replace "constant" with "word" as indicated above.

7.  eliminate "sentence" in the definition for "term"

8.  add "number" to the definition for "term"

9.  add definitions for "relword" and "funword" as follows

relword ::= initialchar wordchar*
funword ::= initialchar wordchar*

replace "constant" with relword and fundword in the definitions for relsent 
and funterm respectively.

10.  Add the sentence "No "relword" and "funword" shall have the same 
characters in a particular knowledge base."

11.  Rename "constant" to "word" in the definition for "sentence" as above.

12.  Delete the definition for "inequality" and references to it.

13.  Replace "*" with "+" in the definitions for "funterm", "relsent" and 
"logsent".

14.  Delete the clause containing "<=" from the definition of "logsent".

15.  Remove the "*" in the clause for "=>" in the definition of "logsent".

*** section 5.1 ***

16.  Remove the reference to "bottom".

*** section 5.2 ***

17.  Remove the reference to "bottom".
