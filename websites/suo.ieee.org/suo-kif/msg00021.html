I have updated the CG standard to incorporate the new CGIF grammar,
but there will be more to come in the next few weeks.  Following
are the two new web pages.

For the version that uses the HTML 4.0 symbols:

   http://www.bestweb.net/~sowa/cg/cgstand.htm

For the version that uses the Adobe symbols font:
 
   http://www.bestweb.net/~sowa/cg/cgstandw.htm

Last week, I attended a workshop on the KIF standard at Texas A & M,
where we discussed many of the issues involved in handling types, sorts,
and metalevel constructs in KIF.  The new version should make it easier
to map KIF to and from CGs and to support equivalent features in both.

For the next two days, I'll be going to the NCITS L8 standards
meeting at NIST (Gaithersburg, MD).  That is the committee that
inherited the standards projects for both KIF and CGs.  Both
Mike Genesereth (the KIF editor) and I (the CG editor) plan to
attend and to restart the two standards projects.

I'll send another note about these developments next week.

John Sowa
