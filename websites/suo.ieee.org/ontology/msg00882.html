Pat,

Well, actually, I was talking about a Lot of the stuff that I have
written here, but if I have to say that then it becomes rather acute
that "What we have here is a failure to communicate", to coin phrase,
and so I am at a Los.  In response to a poignant inquiry from Graham,
I recently supplied a list of all of my notes that I thought had some
hint of at least a single good idea somewhere within the morasses of
their intellectual Sodom and Gomorrah, but that is clearly something
of a Judgment Call.  Anyway, here is a link to my Selective Wrought:

http://grouper.ieee.org/groups/suo/email/msg03000.html

All of the papers that I currently have on the Web are linked here:

http://www.shss.montclair.edu/inquiry/fall95/awbrey.html
http://www.door.net/arisbe/menu/library/aboutcsp/awbrey/integrat.htm
http://www.door.net/arisbe/menu/library/aboutcsp/awbrey/inquiry.htm

The first two arise from joint work with my Immortal Beloved, Susan Awbrey,
and so they have not earned the title to that "absolutely boringly" class
to which I, alone, would be able to elevate them, and any bit of clarity
that they do achieve will be due to the stylistic constraints that this
Gentle Missus imposes, against all odds and will of my own, upon me.

The last essay constitutes the first sixty pages
or so from my work in progress on a dissertation
in Systems Engineering, wherein I am attempting
to integrate all of my earlier explorations in
AI, Computing, Mathematics, and Psychology.
