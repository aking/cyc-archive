Hi Jon, Leo, &Co.
Yes, I think "refinement" is the right word in this connection.
(That's also the term Fabio and I try to formalize in the event 
structures papers, e.g., 
http://www.columbia.edu/~av72/papers/Ndjfl_1996.pdf.)
Actually I think granularity and vagueness should be kept quite 
distinct. Only vagueness, not granularity, yields the heap paradox.
Cheers,
-a
