All,
	It is now clear we need rules for loss of voting rights.  

	How does this sound (not a motion):   "If a voting member fails to
vote (ABSTAIN counts as a vote) in 2 consecutive email ballots, he or she
will no longer have voting rights (and will be informed as such).  Regaining
voting rights will be the same as with new members (submit application and
wait 6-months)."

	I do think people should be able to retain voting rights WITHOUT
being subscribed to the SUO list.  Some may choose to just review documents,
a valuable contribution.   As such, ballots will be sent to the addresses
provided by each voter, plus also to the SUO list.  

Jim Schoening
