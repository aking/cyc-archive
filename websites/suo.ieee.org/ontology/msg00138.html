At the risk of sounding naive, and even optimistic, I would like to
suggest that if the only thing you see us accomplishing in the next
two years is to know that this is a huge project, perhaps we can cut short
the next two years worth of work and just agree right now that it is
big, really BIG, as well as hard, really HARD.

Unfortunately, in my mind, this doesn't get us much closer to our goal.
There are many people who take on large tasks, knowing they are large.
There are plenty of others who see how large a task is, and choose not 
to do it.  It is rare to find those who choose not to take on a task 
helping those who think it worthwhile.

I assume that anyone who is sending mail to this mailing list,
is interested in the existence of a Standard Upper Ontology.
The task is going backward from the goal to the actions necessary
to bring it into existence.
