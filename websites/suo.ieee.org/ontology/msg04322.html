Jon, you wrote:

JA: I'm really trying to get at here is the
    harder facet of abductive sign-formation,
    more the sign's first creation than its
    later use in the acts of noticing, or

This is a helpful, clear statement of a basic intent that
provides purpose for the thrust of your work.  I do find
this very interesting.  I enjoy reading your postings,
often saving them for future reflection.

My own basic intent, which provides purpose for my own
intermittent activity on certain public forums, is the
easier, more mundane facet of clarity in the sending and
receiving.  While acknowleding that this issue of clarity
ultimately traces back to the abductive processes by which
individuals make sense of the worlds that they individually
differentiate, I still believe that immediate progress can
be made by recognizing and calling attention to sources of
ambiguity and misunderstanding between senders and receivers,
and negotiating explicit, conscious agreement on methods to
minimize these sources of ineffective communication.
