MW: You use words like "thing" "real set" and "collection" without

MW: So If I understand you, then I choose collections. I don't see that
as abandoning "real sets" as defined by ZF theory. I was never that much
taken with that view of things. In particular I am not keen on the axiom
of separation. As an extensionalist I do not need to be. There simply is 
no set that is the set of all sets that do not include themselves.

MW: So I am very close to what Chris M recently proposed. I would just 
take a next step and make the sets extensional.
