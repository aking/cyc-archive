MW: Not at least when I am talking formally, as I try to on this list. In
your question above I would have said:

"Do you really never speak of a particular class?"

Of course I know that there are lots of ways that "individual" can be used
quite legitimately, but I know it is a word that can cause a lot of
confusion if not used consistently in this context, and so I have got used
to being disciplined in not using it for other meanings.
