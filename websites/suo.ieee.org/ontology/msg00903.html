Arisbeans, SemioCompagnes, SUO Pursuers, ...

I will now take a break from my break from my break,
which I would be tempted, like many others, to call
a "meta-meta-break" -- I would be tempted, yes, but
I would not, not until I have examined, like not so
many others, the subject of closure in the operator,
the one that may or may not find itself called into
active duty status, out of hypostatic understanding
in reserve, via that "nominal, ostensible, putative"
(NOP) imago of the sign so commonly signed as "meta" --
for what if a meta-meta-break is literally a return? --
would we not then have the equation meta^2 = meta^0,
at least, in application to the theme of this gloss,
that perhaps forgotten subject from which we sprang? --
or if not that, not precisely that, not yet, anyway,
then might we not still be free to find that meta^k,
for some k or another, would return us in good time
to meta^0, the identity operator of the meta series? --
or might it not yet still be permissible, failing a
world that is yet so regular as all of this implies,
that we find ourselves, if not immediately, nor not
periodically, returning to that long lost garden of
even temperament that all the world dubs Equanimity,
then would we not yet still be able to range across
the effabled "Place Of Elision Meadows" (POEM) that
Peirce marked to be the "ultimate reification" (UR),
wherein the "continuity of interrupretations" (COI),
so earnestly to be desired by each blessèd agent of
that selfsame recursèd hint of an eventual messiage,
achieves its final form of closure in meta*whatever?

It Could Happen,

Jon Awbrey
