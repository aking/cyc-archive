Thanks for the pointer to the SIMPLE project.  As time permits, I will try
to mine it and other lexical resources (including Wordnet/EuroWordNet) for
concepts and relations that we might want to include in the merged ontology.
The reason that I didn't refer to these resources in the first two versions
of the ontology is that I wanted a philosophically rigorous conceptual
skeleton upon which we could hang more specific content, and one limitation
of linguistically motivated ontologies is that they often contain concepts
that are not precisly defined or that violate non-circularity constraints.
However, I for one think that these ontologies will be essential for
fleshing out the skeleton that we are in the process of refining.
