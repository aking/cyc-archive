A recent legal ruling in Germany implicitly puts the question of ownership of metadata in the limelight: in a recent high court ruling it was decided that trademarks used as either a name or value anywhere in html metadata fall under the protection of the copyright and/or trademark laws.

In principle this follows current jurisprudence in protecting domain names, but the problem is that ownership is implicitly being claimed and supported for a definition, i.e. a lexical term its ontological basis. This raises all sorts of problems.

Are there any similar cases in other countries?
