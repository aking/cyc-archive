A -> ~A -> B

(  A  (
( (A) ( B
))))

    A
    o
    |
    o--o B
A   |
o---o
|
@

same as A => [A or B]

i forget peirce's exact names for the rules,
but there's one that i call "weed & seed"
that lets you delete the higher A above,
in the context of the lower A, getting:

    o
    |
    o--o B
A   |
o---o
|
@

then there's one that i call "dominance"
that lets you get to here:

    o
    |
    o
A   |
o---o
|
@

cancellation (or reflexion) gets this:

A
o---o
|
@

dominance again gets this:

o---o
|
@

cancellation again gets this:

@

which is tree-form for the blank sheet of assertion,
interpreted as true in the existential interpretation.
