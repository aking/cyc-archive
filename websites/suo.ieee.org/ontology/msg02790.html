Jean-Marc,

You and I have scattered the words like stars across the stygian void:
accident, collection, detail, distance, essence, incident, individual,
inquiry, particular, position, semiosis, substance, an even dozen lot
of categories to rival the starry-eyed if not the gods.  Do we really
grasp their meaning, or does their reach exceed our charts and graphs?
And what of their bearing on the earth below, and how are they kin to
the pluralities of affects, experiences, feelings, hearts, minds, and
senses that we bear?  Do you actually imagine that I would cleave the
wonder of this beautiful child, that I'll once and forever be, in two?
Guess again, and again, and still again, for your birth gives you the
right to cast the guesses like stars into the skies of one brief life.

Fare Thee Well,

Jon Awbrey
