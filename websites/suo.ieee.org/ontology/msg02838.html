Jim --
   I think the criterion you suggest for loss of voting rights
is too stringent -- I would prefer that a voting member be
required to vote (possibly by abstention) on
a majority of ballots over some three-month period,
and a probaytionary period be then instituted before
voting rights are terminated.  Also, I think that it should
be easier to resume voting rights once terminated than
to acquire them initially.

    Pat Cassidy
