SIGH SIG,

John Sowa alluded to the undeniable circumstance --
for who, indeed, would you deny it to!? -- that our
minimal "community of interpretation" -- definitely
not to our COI mistress, after all!? -- consists in
two parts -- though I observe no rules against both
roles being played at turns by the very same player,
still, hush, I know that he is perfectly aware that
Peirce himself wasted no breath on the fantasy of a
private language, as many less sanguine and lacking
in savvy might on occasion have been tempted to wit.

So even a Hermit on an Ill-Deserted Desert-Island ...
And per shape most especially one just alike that ...
Hast that "Dialogue In Nature" (DIN) to entertain ...
And that "Animated Intercourse Of Nurture" (AION) ...
To maintain twixt the "Nature Of Our Soul" (NOOS) ...

But I promised you a game that would be rawer and
gamier than this "Hunt And Pick" (HAPax Legomenon) --
so here 'tis:
