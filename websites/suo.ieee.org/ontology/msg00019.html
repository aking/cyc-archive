MW: Yes, I quite agree (I think at least). Establishing what others mean
when they use words is an inevitable part of any communication process, and
has to be gone through pretty much when anyone new enters the conversation.
I take this for granted. My way of overcoming this is to see things not as
isolated, but as part of a network, so that whilst the word itself can be
used as one means of identifying a possible meaning, it is the network of
relationships, and agreement on those that is the real arbiter.

Am I missing anything?
