For future reference, I give here the sixteen propositional forms on
two logical variables, depicted or expressed in four different ways.
Also, I list two collections of conventional nicknames that I will
use for these functions:  the "F" series codes the truth table for
the proposition as a binary numeral in its subscript, the "f" series
employs the decimal equivalent of this binary numeral as its subscript.
An explanation of the text string expressions and the graphical forms
in the second column can be found in the archives of the CG List:

http://www.virtual-earth.de/CG/cg-list/msg03351.html
