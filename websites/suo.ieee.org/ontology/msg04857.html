Differential of Conjunction (concl.)

Figures 46-a through 46-d illustrate the proposition dJ, rounded out
in our usual array of prospects.  This proposition of EU% is what we
refer to as the (first order) differential of J, and normally regard
as 'the' differential proposition corresponding to J.
