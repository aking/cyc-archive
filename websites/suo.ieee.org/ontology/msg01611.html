Dear Jim,

In SC4 we use a numbering system like ISO/TC184/SC4/WG10/N123. I guess this
would translate for us to something like IEEE/SUO/N123.

You might like to look at the WG10 Document list, that fulfils a similar
purpose and which has been established for a number of years and I know is
much appreciated.
http://www.nist.gov/sc4/wg_qc/wg10/current/

It includes things like sort by author, document number, or title, and has
links for things like "supercedes" and "superceded by" which is important
when you are developing standards.
