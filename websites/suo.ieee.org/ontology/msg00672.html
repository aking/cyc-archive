Matthew et al,  
	.		We have here an actual proposal for another start
point. I suspect it already has a name, and would be one of the references
to which we were referred at the start of the project. I further suspect
there are other such options, which could also be considered, either from
that list, or that have been mentioned by some participants since. Of
course, we still have Ian Niles' product. 

	.	The big question is, how much support do we have for any of
them? 

	.	Hopefully we will find an option that will give us a
positive way forward. This requires two things: 
*	majority support for the one of the candidate starting points, and 
*	a viable mechanism for the development of the starting point into a
satisfactory product for submission to the formal standards approval
process.
