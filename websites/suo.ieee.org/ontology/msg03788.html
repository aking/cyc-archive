| Air, en l' [ahn lehr].
| In the air.  Indicates:  (1) that a movement is to be
| made in the air;  for example, rond de jambe en l'air;
| (2) that the working leg, after being opened to the
| second or fourth position à terre, is to be raised
| to a horizontal position with the toe on the level
| of the hip.
|
| Alignment.
| See Directions or body alignment.
|
| Gail Grant,
|'Technical Manual And Dictionary Of Classical Ballet',
| Third Revised Edition, Dover, New York, NY, 1982.
