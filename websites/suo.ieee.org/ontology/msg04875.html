Transformations of Type B^2 -> B^2 (cont.)

Figure 63 give a more complete picture of the transformation F,
showing how the points of U% are transformed into points of X%.
The lines that cross from one universe to the other trace the
action that F induces on points, in other words, they depict
the aspect of the transformation that acts as a mapping from
points to points, and chart its effects on the elements that
are variously called cells, points, positions, or singular
propositions.
