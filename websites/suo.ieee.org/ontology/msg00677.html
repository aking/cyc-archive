The underlying idea of having a solid foundatin is sound. However, I am
not sure that 'philosophically rigorous' is a good criteria for a solid
foundation. It needs to be semantically clear, logially well defined, etc

as it helps to achieve our goals, but I'm not one to place philosophically rigor
at the forefront of our consideratations.  It may be that I'm just mincing
words and am missing the point. If so, I apologize.
