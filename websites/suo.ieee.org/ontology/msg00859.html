Pat and All,

	A 'copyright release' is not giving away the copyright, it's just
allowing someone else to  modify it, copyright the new version, and
distribute it. Cyc would still retain their original copyright. 
 
	 I talked to Doug Lenat and he seemed very open to submitting his
Cyc Upper Ontology to SUO (with a signed copyright release letter), but he
hasn't sent in the signed letter.

	Companies (including Microsoft) do this all the time, so let's not
assume we know what Cyc would determine to be in their business interests. 

	Below is the template for a copyright release letter.

Jim Schoening
