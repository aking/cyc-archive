SUO,

	As there seem to be no further objections, the following SUO WG
Voting Rights Clause is considered approved.  Please speak up if anyone
objects to this conclusion.  I have made minor edits to the clause so it
doesn't become dated.

	All those interested in SUO voting rights should pull down the
simple spreadsheet application from at
http://suo.ieee.org/SUO-Participants-Jan2001.xls, fill it out, and mail it
to ME by March 2, 2001.  Please, do NOT post it to this list. 

	This is the same exact information and spreadsheet required by IEEE,
so this isn't collecting any more information than we would otherwise need
to obtain. This information will not be posted on the web.


Jim Schoening

===========================================================

SUO Voting Rights Clause:
 
 	Voting rights shall be granted to any individual with a direct and
material interest in the development of this standard who either was a
participant of this group during its formation (see para. c.) or has been a
participant for a waiting period of 6-months.  
 
 	a. 'Voting Rights' refers to the list of who may vote on formal
ballots taken by the SUO WG.  If an individual does not have voting rights,
he or she may still submit comments on a vote and participate in discussions
to resolve those comments.

 	b. A 'participant' is any individual (IEEE membership NOT required
or expected) who submits an application (see
http://suo.ieee.org/SUO-Participants-Jan2001.xls or SUO web site at
http://suo.ieee.org) and participates in any one of the following ways
(subscribes to any SUO discussion list, posts messages to lists, comments on
documents, or any other actions the Chair deems to be participation).

 	c. Voting rights will be granted to all those who participated in
this WG prior to Jan 9, 2001 and who submit an application form by March 2,
2001. New participants must submit the application and then wait 6-months.
 
End of Clause.
=============================
 
Jim Schoening
