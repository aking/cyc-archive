In distinguishing a knowledge base from a data model, I'm drawing attention
to the fact that the former is not specificaly intended to be the basis forf
expressing date, the latter is. The former is usually intended to be
a basis for providing inference, the latter is not.
I hope this helps.  Defining  what a 'knowledge base' is, is not a
happy enterprise...
