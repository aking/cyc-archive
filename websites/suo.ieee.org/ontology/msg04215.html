But that's not the situation my example has been addressing.  There's no 
systematic process, just an individual data entry error.  Note that there 
may be thousands of such entirely random errors created by tired data entry 
clerks.

> >

>you run on the whole database periodically.

Eureka!  And that periodic database check would be one way to use a formal 
ontology, doing data cleaning, checking for data entry typos, against 
constraints like and individual's birth must come before his or her death.
