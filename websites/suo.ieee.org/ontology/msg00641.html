Hello SUO list members,

FYI I am helping to organize the following event in Hong Kong in April 
(24-28) about the relevance of database semantics and ontologies, in this 
happenstance for the e-commerce domain. It is the 9th edition of the Data 
Semantics conference (started DS-1 in '85 when nobody had heard of database 
semantics). Appends conveniently to WWW10 too this year. Details on the 
website (program is by refereed contributions, deadline is past, selection 
ongoing and nearly complete).

http://www.comp.polyu.edu.hk/ds9/

Hope to see some of you there!
