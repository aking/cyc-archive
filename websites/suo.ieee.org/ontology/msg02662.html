  To be more specific, the book is called "The End of Time"
by Julian Barbour, published by Oxford University Press, 2000.
   After reading the first 70 of 350 pages, I am tempted
to suggest that Barbour is neither perdurantist nor endurantist by
adurantist.  It is definitely a fascinating theory, and I will be
interested to see where he goes with it, but so far I haven't
seen any evidence that this approach will resolve any of our
disagreements about the SUO.
