May I put in a plea for leading "0"s in the numbering system, as most software will treat these as strings and not sort properly.  At the rate that our e-mails are going we will reach more than a 1000 documents in not too many years (as some SC4 Working Groups have done).  I am just not sure whether we should go for four or five digits ;-)
