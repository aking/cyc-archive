Good question. I just saw a talk by some OntoMap folk at an Ontologies and Agents workshop here in Montreal. See all papers at: http://cis.otago.ac.nz/OASWorkshop/Papers/paper_list.html 

It was very interesting and very ambitious, but it was not clear to me where they intended to get traction on the many difficult problems that ramain unsolved in this area.  I was unable to have a longer chat. They seem to be wanting to create a service for mapping between different UOs rather than developing a UO.  I asked whether they were familiar with the IEEE SUO group, and they said no.
