For Peirce's own presentation of his existential graphs and
related topics, see his MS 514 with my commentary that relates
it to what else was happening at the same time:

   http://www.bestweb.net/~sowa/peirce/ms514.htm

This is a good tutorial on Peirce's rules of inference as
applied to EGs (and with minor modifications to CGs).
