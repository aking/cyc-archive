Jon,  I would agree that logic is normative to the extent that to reason
otherwise is not effective.  But mostly, in my view, because logic
desribes the way one must reason to accord with the way the world
actually is.  But I appreciate your pointing this out as frankly for a
moment there (while putting this together) I could not even think of what
logic was generally considered to be (i.e. correct or effective
reasoning).  I mean I got so narrowly focused on the notion I was trying
to explore. I think that sometimes the questions of beginners, such as
myself, are the most challenging.  So I appreciate your time and
patience.
