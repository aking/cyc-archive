Jon,

>Sadly, until our computers get to understand the way we talk,
>with all of these figures of speech, metaphor, metonymy, and
>many more, somebody will have to do the dirty job of getting
>them to grok it.

Yes, that is true.  But in a great many cases, metonymy can
be recognized.  In fact, it is widely used in programming
languages under the name of "coercion."

Metonymy, although potentially confusing in some cases, is
extremely valuable in making a limited vocabulary apply to
an unlimited range of applications.  I highly recommend it.
