Be careful what you wish for. Here's a sample of what you might get. It is a
direct English rendering of the KIF version of Casati and Varsi definition
of 'surface', from Niles' Merge.txt  document, simplified slightly to make
it more readable. :

"Something is the surface of something else if and only if the first thing
is self-connected and it is a superfical part of the second thing and also
if everything which is self-connected and a superficial part of the second
thing and also is connected to the first thing is a part of the first thing.
"

I would guess that what you really want is not an English translation, but
an English *explanation*.
