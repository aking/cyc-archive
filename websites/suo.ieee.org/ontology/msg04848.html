Tacit Extension of Conjunction (cont.)

Figures 37-a through 37-d present several pictures of the proposition J and
its tacit extension !e!J.  Notice in these Figures how !e!J in EU% visibly
extends J in U%, by annexing to the indicated cells of J all of the arcs
that exit from or flow out of them.  In effect, this extension attaches
to these cells all of the dispositions that spring from them, in other
words, it attributes to these cells all of the conceivable changes
that are their issue.
