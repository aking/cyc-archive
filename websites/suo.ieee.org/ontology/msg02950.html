I will follow, as best I can, the advice of Paul,
and try to form a more perfect exposition on the
topic of "Hypostatic Abstraction" (HA), which,
were I to have my druthers, though I never do,
I would pose as "Abstractive Hypostasis" (AH).

At least these names, henceforth acronymized as AH and HA,
as pragmaticians are wont to say, "are ugly enough to be
safe from kidnappers", even if their acronymphs are not.
If this has the effect to weaken the erstwhile and fast
and good and honored associations among "hypostasis",
"person", "reflection", "reification", "subjection",
"substance", "supposition", "thematization", usw,
well, 'tis not my doing that did it so.

This Anchor Forged,
I Let It Drop Here,
And Cast Dies Irae,
Fora While To Anon.

Jon Awbrey
