	A couple of weeks ago I sent out a merged ontology that incorporates
many of the candidate sources for the SUO.  As I mentioned in the
accompanying email, my hope is that this ontology can function as a very
rough draft of our eventual SUO.   Pat Hayes was kind enough to provide
detailed comments on this ontology, and, with his permission, I am attaching
a copy of the ontology interspersed with his comments (in upper case) and my
comments (in red) on his comments.  Any further remarks on these comments or
on the original ontology would be greatly appreciated.  By the end of this
week I'll be sending out a new version of the ontology that incorporates
many of Pat Hayes' suggestions, as well as some new sources.
