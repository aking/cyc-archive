JA: Short answer:  To the extent that a concrete syntax for FOL is built on
    the foundation of a concrete syntax for ZOL, and to the extent that the
    underlying concrete foundation is poured from the conglomerate mixer of
    PABloid logic, the entire superstructure of the logical edifice that is
    erected on this site will continue to rest -- if you can call that rest --
    on the fault lines of a radically undermind neural plate architectonics.
    Furthermore, the PABlematic foundation presents a practical obstacle to
    constructing several other very useful, almost indispensable extensions
    to the basic infrastructure of ZOL, like the differential extension and
    the higher order propositional extension, which latter affords the mind
    with novel views on the vistas of a generalized quantificational logic.
