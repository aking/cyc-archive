Hi Graham,

.	David Whitten is responsible for the structural ontology.  Chris
Menzel and I have converted five of the SUO sources into a SUO-KIF format
which is compliant with this structural ontology (these five sources are
John Sowa's upper ontology, Casati and Varzi's formal theory of holes, Barry
Smith's formal theory of boundaries, Borgo, Guarino, and Masolo's formal
theory of physical objects, and the Core Plan Representation).  In addition,
Allen's temporal logic and Russell and Norvig's upper ontology had
previously been translated into KIF.  All of these sources/translations can
be accessed at:

 http://ltsc.ieee.org/suo/refs.html

.	Currently, I am working on merging all of these sources into a
single,
coherent ontology, which I plan to release to the group at the end of this
week.  I'm hoping that this merge can function as the bare beginning of our
eventual SUO.  Furthermore, Adam Pease and I are working on converting the
ITBM-CNR ontologies and the ontologies on the Ontolingua server into SUO-KIF
format.  Once this conversion is complete, we'll make it available in some
form.  

	If you can suggest an appropriate source for biological/medical
concepts, I would be happy to investigate its translation into SUO-KIF.

Sincerely,
Ian Niles
