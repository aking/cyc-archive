Of course. On the other hand, of course, that would entail that the 
god had created the god, among other things (since some things appear 
to clearly be something other than the god; I am thinking in 
particular of one of my uncles); and since it seems hard to see quite 
how something could have created itself as well as other things, it 
would seem to follow that the premis in question is likely to be 
false. Not, of course, that that conclusion should come as any 
particular surprise, under the circumstances, since both of the signs 
in question here - "the god" and "this feeling of 'q'"  - both seem 
to be prime  examples of non-referring strings. Which brings us to 
the central question which this message raises: why are we even 
bothing to read this rubbish?
