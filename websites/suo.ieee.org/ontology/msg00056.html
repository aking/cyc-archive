	Attached is a SUO-KIF formalization (done by Chris Menzel) of Casati
and Varzi's formal theory of holes, outlined in HOLES AND OTHER
SUPERFICIALITIES, MIT Press, 1995.  The idea is that this formalization,
along with the other KIF-ified ontology sources, will eventually be folded
into the SUO.  Any questions, criticisms or comments regarding this
formalization or the original content would be greatly appreciated.
