Returning to our last rough attempt to synthesize the
ontological, the semiotic, and the systems pictures
of the world, we arrive at a scheme like this:

                                  o  o  o  Properties
                                   ^ ^ ^
                                    \|/
                                     o     Sign
Properties  o  o  o               : /|\
             ^ ^ ^             :   v v v
              \|/           :     o  o  o  Instances
    Object     o<::::::::@
              /|\           :     o  o  o  Properties
             v v v             :   ^ ^ ^
 Instances  o  o  o               : \|/
                                     o     Interpretant
                                    /|\
                                   v v v
                                  o  o  o  Instances

The double dotted 3-adic relation "@" represents a sign relation by way of
one of its 3-tuples.  The object, sign, interpretant domains all have some
sort of "ontological" ordering that is defined on them.  I have gone ahead
and labeled all of these in accordance with the "property-instance" genre,
but it is more than likely that we will be interested in other sorts of
compositional ("part-whole") series in at least some of these domains.

Spots before my eyes -- have to take a break ...
