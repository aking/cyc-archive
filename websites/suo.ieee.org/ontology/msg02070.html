Hello everybody,

I wonder if I can temporarily divert you from the current topic.

For some time I have been toying with the idea of starting a new teaching
course ('module', as we call it here) aimed at the analysis and generation
of ontologies for relevant real-world domains.

I hope I can use the collective (and individual) wisdom of the SUO group to
help formulate a syllabus for this course. It would be either an
undergraduate final-year option, or an M.Sc. (i..e. taught, postgraduate)
level, involving some 36 hours of teaching, tutorials or practicals.

I would like to draw on our work in CGs, but want to open the topics as
widely as possible (within reason).

I would welcome any suggestions for the syllabus (and inevitable lively
discussion).

Thanks,
