The dotted lines indicate denotative, referential,
or semantic relations between signs and objects.

The dashed lines indicate connotative or semiotic
relations between signs and interpretant signs.
Each part of the partition of the syntax domain
then graphs as a digraph with a loop at each
point and arcs in both directions between.
