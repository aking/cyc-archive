MW: In EPISTLE we have a top level (upper upper) 4D ontology. It is
currently being edited to be issued as a Draft International Standard by
ISO, so it is somewhat further forward and more coherent than our fumblings
here. Unfortunately, it is written in an entity relationship language called
EXPRESS (never mind that this is already an ISO standard) and is long on
structure and short on axioms. I have translated part of this into KIF
around the beginning of November. This is very difficult for me. As I am
sure you are aware, writing well in a language for a newcomer is much harder
than reading. It was anyway largely ignored, and now appears to be forgotten
as well, but please do not accuse me of not proposing something more solid. 

GH>	I am working on the premise that, when Jim called for the vote, that
it doesn't consider previous proposals. Hence, if you still believe the
option has merit, I'd encourage you to re-state it, possibly pointing out
that you proposed it before. You may note that I have today called for all
pre-existing ontologies to be reviewed for relevance, etc., as part of the
project anyway.
