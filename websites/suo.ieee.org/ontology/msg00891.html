True Story:

I once suggested to a few proponents of "genetic algorithms"
that they ought to try writing a brand new word processor by
mixing together all of the codes from their favorite exemplars
into the same evolutionary niche where they would be permitted,
as I said, to "have sex with each other", that is to say, where
they would be subjected to a process of "genetic cross-over" --
well, they actually took me seriously, and excitedly started
discussing how they would go about implementing the scheme.
I tend to suspect that all of them now have big fat jobs
at MicroSoft making a zillion more bucks that I will
ever see in my life.  Anyway, it would explain a lot.

Oh well ...
