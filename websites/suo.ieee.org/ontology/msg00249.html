Hi All,

	Attached is a first attempt to merge some of the SUO sources into a
single, coherent ontology.  As I've mentioned in previous emails, my hope is
that this merge can function as the first sketch of our eventual SUO.  The
merge incorporates David Whitten's structural ontology, John Sowa's upper
ontology, Allen's temporal logic, Russell and Norvig's upper ontology,
Casati and Varzi's formal theory of holes, Barry Smith's formal theory of
boundaries, Borgo, Guarino, and Masolo's formal theory of physical objects,
the Core Plan Representation, and the Agents and Numbers ontologies from the
Ontolingua server (all of the original sources can be viewed at
http://ltsc.ieee.org/suo/refs.html).  

	My attempt to merge these sources is bound to contain many
inaccuracies and gaps, so all comments, criticisms, etc. would be greatly
appreciated.  

Sincerely,
Ian Niles
