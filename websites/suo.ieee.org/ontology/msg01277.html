	The deadline for submitting your application for voting rights in
SUO is March 2, 2001.  If you miss this deadline, you will have to submit
your application and wait 6-months before being able to vote.

from 
 <http://suo.ieee.org/SUO-Participants-Jan2001.xls> It is not an online
form.  You have to download, save, and mail it back as an attachment.
