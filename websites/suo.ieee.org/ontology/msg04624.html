PLG.  Exegetical Note 1

o~~~~~~~~~o~~~~~~~~~o~~~~~~~~~o~~~~~~~~~o~~~~~~~~~o

Shakespeare's 'Measure for Measure' is about a fatal decision.
It's a decision that's fatal for one man, but made by another.
The dramatic dynamic of the play involves the intercession of
the nuncial Isabella who is charged and determined to mediate
the awareness of a reciprocity that each of us owes the other,
in the interval before its execution to stay the executionary
force of this sentence.

Jon Awbrey
