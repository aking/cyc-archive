hi martin,

it seems i am the first one up this morning,
so i will just pass along the following
info on the ieee suo wg mailing lists:

what i always do to subscribe or to unsubscribe --
we don't have a "holiday", "no maul", or "vacation"
setting, so it is necessary to on and off a lot --
is just to send the following sorts of commands to:

<majordomo@ieee.org>

for example,
some subset of the following,
according to your wont at the time:

subscribe standard-upper-ontology
subscribe ontology
subscribe suo-kif
subscribe suo-ce
end

unsubscribe standard-upper-ontology
unsubscribe ontology
unsubscribe suo-kif
unsubscribe suo-ce
end

this should do it under normal circumstances.
