Each episod shows enunciative links between selected disks (as starting
terms, with anaphore)
in 1-2, disk vert is located to disk rose (connective pour qu'après), and
the modal verb 'puisse' appear for the move of disk 2 which is in the
background ; the same thing appears in 6, in 9 and in 10

        I interpreted modal verbs as pointing to an intended goal. It points
to the fact that the participant has constructed the possibility of another
state and that he made a decision. This in contrast with the case where
there is no modal verb, and where the plan is automatically recalled from
environmental cues, or from data stored in memory. 
What is interesting to note is the fact that first modal verbs appear with
first locations at enunciative level. This trial 2 shows very complex
relations which have nothing to do with a recursive strategy.
