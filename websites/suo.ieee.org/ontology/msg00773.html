Peter,

Welcome To Our Band Of Merry Players!

If it's Contention you're after,
You have arrived at Contentment!
So let me Contend to play along,
In seeking a Common Contentment,
If not to mention, still to use,
My sense of a more perfect Fit.

I will "see" the first part
of your contention, and then
bid to "raise" you, at least,
in the inverse sense of vying
to underwhelm you, one and all,
with the zeroth part of my own
contention, to wit, as it were,
quite subjunctively on purpose,
that this enfabled relationskip
of objects to signs, "of" which,
but also "in" which we do speak,
is verily, or similar, at least,
at first, Many-To-Many, and our
job of Job, in a biblical sense,
is just to attempt, like I said,
all in good time, to remedy the
vast muddle of it by just a bit.
