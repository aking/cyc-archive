Neither for the sake of Gumping or Trumping any persons present,
but simply on behalf of those of us who still remember that for
which that late, great "Information Revolution" (IR) was fought,
let us pause for a moment to praise famous gnomes and all exert
our collectively stored but our distributively dimming memories
to try and remember that once-and-for-all-important distinction
between a string of mere signs and a true parcel of information.

Let "No Syntaxation Without Representation!" Now & Forever Be Our Banner,
And Never Be Regarded So Raggedly-Taggedly As Yet Another Rag Of Chiffon!

Jon Awbrey
