Matthew,
   KIF is so expressive that it would yield a grammar equivalent to 
English.  Conversely, English is so expressive that a full grammar would be 
huge and translation to logic impractical (hence the difficulty in state of 
the art natural language understanding). The only feasible way I know to 
begin is to start with a very small grammar and build up gradually.
