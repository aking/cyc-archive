Change SIG,

Let me try to state my position in yet another way, through
the use of a certain analogy between physics and metaphysics --
the last time that I checked, anyway, the Scene that the whole
world calls "Ontology" was still seen to be set on the Stage
that the very same audience hails and hoots as "Metaphysics" --
so let me now ask you the following question:

"Where is change handled in physics?"

Trick question, trick handle --

The handle is gripped outside the container.
Just so, the handles that we have on dynamics,
to wit, the pan-potentials to change in physics,
are those forms, those all too discontential forms
that we discover to hang on the pegs of mathematics.

So?

So maybe, just maybe, the way that we will, in Time,
find ourselves forced to cope with Change, and also
to deal with its equally uncouth cousin, Diversity,
even as those two love-born and strife-torn twins
play out their many-splintered and sundry motifs
in the episodes and the interludes of this stage,
Our Own Ontology (OOO), might be, ever resurgently
transmuted here, to undertake its handling within the
regime that out-&-over-&-under-lies Metaphysics wholly,
where none other lies that I might mean but that which
takes "Logic" as its very own familiar and truest name.

And I will, anon, in another frame of mind,
show you a few of the more prosaic details
of how we might begin to play out this Act.

Until Then,

Jon Awbrey
