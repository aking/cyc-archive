Matthew,

Since I do not view your position as philosophically or logically adequate
to the full range of human experience, I am trying to locate the virtue of
your position, which seems to have been well thought out, by construing it
as adequate some to more limited range of human experience-- in this case,
experience with trying to represent a portion of experience within the
limitations of relational databases. I thought that this was also the
virtue that Frederick Chase saw in it. 

Lee
