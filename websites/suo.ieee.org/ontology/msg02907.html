The game is not an imaginary one:
The game consists of three aligned pegs: a, b, c, and of four discs of
different colours and size, placed  from the large bottom one to the tiny
top one on the hook a. The purpose of the game is to place all the discs on
the peg c, in the same order by respecting both following constraints: 
- Move only a single disc at the same moment
- Do not put a largest disc on one smaller one.
One asks to the participant to verbalize in order to say what he thinks what
he is doing. The
  verbalizations are recorded and actions are noted. 
The aim is to reconstitute the strategy of problem solving from
verbalizations and actions.
