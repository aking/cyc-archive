The position you describe for Ian is called a "Technical Editor" in IEEE parlance.  It is roughly equivalent to the ISO/IEC position called "Project Editor".  An IEEE Technical Editor is the "keeper of the pen", i.e., he/she is a subject area expert who takes direction from the committee and merges changes into the document.
