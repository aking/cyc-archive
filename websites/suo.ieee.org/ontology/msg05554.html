***********************************************************
         ON THE MOVE FEDERATED (OTM) 2007 WORKSHOPS

http://www.cs.rmit.edu.au/fedconf/index.html?page=workshops

Proceedings will be published by Springer Verlag
***********************************************************
Vilamoura, Algarve, Portugal 25-30 November, 2007
***********************************************************

The current and future software needs are towards the development
of large and complex Intelligent Networked Information Systems,
covering a wide range of issues (such as Data and Web Semantics,
Distributed Objects, Web Services, Databases, Workflow, Cooperation,
Interoperability, Mobility) as required for the deployment of
Internet- and Intranet-based systems in organizations and for
e-business.

OTM 2007 Workshops cover important Information Technology areas,
such as:

  . Agents and Web Services in Distributed Environments (AWeSOMe)
    http://www.cs.rmit.edu.au/fedconf/index.html?page=awesome2007cfp
  . Context-Aware Mobile Services (CAMS)
    http://www.cs.rmit.edu.au/fedconf/index.html?page=cams2007cfp
  . Community Informatics (COMINF)
    http://www.cs.rmit.edu.au/fedconf/index.html?page=cominf2007cfp
  . MObile and NEtworking Technologies for social applications (MONET)
    http://www.cs.rmit.edu.au/fedconf/index.html?page=monet2007cfp
  . Networking for Distributed Knowledge Management (NDKM)
    http://www.cs.rmit.edu.au/fedconf/index.html?page=ndkm2007cfp
  . Ontology content and evaluation in Enterprise (OnToContent)
    http://www.cs.rmit.edu.au/fedconf/index.html?page=onto2007cfp
  . Object-Role Modeling (ORM)
    http://www.cs.rmit.edu.au/fedconf/index.html?page=orm2007cfp
  . Pervasive Systems (PerSys)
    http://www.cs.rmit.edu.au/fedconf/index.html?page=persys2007cfp
  . Privacy in Pervasive Environments (PiPE)
    http://www.cs.rmit.edu.au/fedconf/index.html?page=pipe2007cfp
  . Peer to Peer Networks (PPN)
    http://www.cs.rmit.edu.au/fedconf/index.html?page=ppn2007cfp
  . Reliability in Decentralized Distributed Systems (RDDS)
    http://www.cs.rmit.edu.au/fedconf/index.html?page=rdds2007cfp
  . Scalable Semantic Web Knowledge Base Systems (SSWS)
    http://www.cs.rmit.edu.au/fedconf/index.html?page=ssws2007cfp
  . Semantic Web & Web Semantics (SWWS)
    http://www.cs.rmit.edu.au/fedconf/index.html?page=swws2007cfp
  . Doctoral Consortium
    http://www.cs.rmit.edu.au/fedconf/index.html?page=dc2007cfp

All these workshops are intended for researchers and practioners
working in any of these areas who want to interact with other
people working in the same field or to enrich their research with
ideas coming from complementary research areas.

SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS

All submitted papers will be carefully evaluated based on their
originality, significance, technical soundness, and clarity of
expression. All submissions must be in English. Submissions should be in
PDF format and must not exceed 10 pages in the final camera-ready
format. Authors instructions can be found at:
http://www.springer.de/comp/lncs/authors.html.

IMPORTANT DATES FOR OTM'07 WORKSHOPS

Abstract Submission Deadline		July 20, 2007
Paper Submission Deadline		July 26, 2007
Acceptance Notification			September 1, 2007
Camera Ready Due			September 10, 2007
Registration Due			September 10, 2007
Conference				November 25-30, 2007
