PRE 2.  Peripatus

o~~~~~~~~~o~~~~~~~~~o~~~~~~~~~o~~~~~~~~~o~~~~~~~~~o

There are times when I run --- And run into it.

There are times when I walk --- And walk into it.

There are times when I run --- And run in the dark,

    And then I run into it ... In the dark.

There are times when I walk --- And walk in the light,

    And then I walk up to it ... In the light.

But every time I come to it, there it is, that boundary.

Jon Awbrey, Beethoven's Birthday, 16 Dec 2003
