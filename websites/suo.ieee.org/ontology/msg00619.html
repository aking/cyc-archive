Dear Robert,

I appreciate you can do this, but you are then dealing at a "meta-ontology"
level, and I rather understood this was something we were advised to avoid.

There is always a choice between what you implement in the language and what
you implement with the language. I can imagine that many users of KIF would
want to do the sort of thing I describe, not just us. It would therefor
seema shame to encourage the position where each developes their own
slightly differnet meta-ontology for something they would all need. My view
is that these things are really better done in the language itself.

Regards
