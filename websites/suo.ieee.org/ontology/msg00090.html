Dear Adam,

I think you are missing what I hope Pat's point is: i.e. there are a number
of world viewpoints out there, and that even if we did come up with some
particular choice of world viewpoint, this would be of little use until we
had understood its relationship to the existing world viewpoints out there.

Choice between viewpoints is bad, understanding relationships between
viewpoints is good.
