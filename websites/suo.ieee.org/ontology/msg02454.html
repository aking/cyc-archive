With one of my PHD student, I have just submitted a paper to the ARCo
Colloque I organize in Lyon next December. I think it could perhaps interest
you. It concerned the problem of the structuration of agregates during a
problem solving (ToH).
        I try to explain you the main points :
- at a first level -of the situation - locations appear with prepositions in
the case of the tower of hanoï problem, the criterium stand in the
repetition of peg or repetition of disk
example 1   disk3 on peg B
          disk 1 on peg B

example 2  disk 3 on peg B
           disk 1 on peg 3

It starts a local integration and structuration of moves and disks

- at another level (enunciative level), starting terms appear (cf the paper
you already know), and also direct relations between starting terms

- but an intermediary level occured, when starting terms are not yet linked
in a direct way -ie at this level-, and when they are only linked through
the situationnel level

        These three levels seems to me very important in order to give an
account of the interaction of participant with physical environnement, i.e.
of the cognitive elaboration and structuration of agregates
Furthermore at each level processes of integration of elements and of
demarcating of episodes can be showed.
Moreover, the situational level seems to constitute a sort of context for
the enunciative level
