<s<1>, ..., s<k>> is a Proof of Fermat's Lost Theorem

if and only if

There exists a p of type Proving such that
p is a Proving of Fermat's Lost Theorem &
Proj<j>(p) = s<j> for all j = 1 to k.

I believe that settles the matter conclusively.
