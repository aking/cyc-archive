Transformations of Type B^2 -> B^2 (cont.)

Table 67 shows how to compute the analytic series
for F = <f, g> = <((u)(v)), ((u, v))> in terms of
coordinates, and Table 68 recaps these results in
symbolic terms, agreeing with earlier derivations.
