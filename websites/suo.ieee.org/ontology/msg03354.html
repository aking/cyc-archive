What does that mean, 'sharing experience'?
Does it mean that 'some original pathema' (SOP),
some impression of the 'world as a whole' (WAAW),
is apportioned into lots and distributed to our
several rations, here, on this earthly sphere?
Something to muse on while I drone.

Having the incidence matrix of -- what shall I name it for short? -- "Lot(2^2^2)"
before me, I am emboldened to try and sketch its lattice diagram in Asciiischean.
In the diagram, the name "j", coded in Hexadecimal, will denote the function f_j.
By way of laying out the lattice of Lot(16) a bit more elegantly in the frame of
the plane, let me exploit a trick of Peirce, known as the "teridentity relation",
in order to identify the scattered avatars of entities in the order whose tokens
are required to be distributed throughout a host of different sites in the plane,
all the more facilely to meet the demands of their various and sundry incidents.
