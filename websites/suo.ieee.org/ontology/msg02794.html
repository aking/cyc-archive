COMMENTS

1 - Every solid disk gives raise to a selected disk.

2 - Demarcation of episods :
- at situational level : with a change in the denomination of peg in 18, 21,
30  (in 4, 6, 10 and 22 there are locations on the solid disk associated
with selected disks; so there is not demarcation of episods)
- at enunciative level : four episods whose separation appear with
connective 'then' in 3, 23, 28. E1 can be decomposed in two sub-episods in
18 and E4 in 31 with the change of denomination of selected disk

3 - locations

- at situational level : 
location on solid disk in 2-4;  5-6 ; 7-10;   20-21
locations on the peg in 11-12;  13-14;  32-33;  36-37

- at enunciative level :
only juxtaposition of disks and juxtaposition of moves

- at intermediate level : selected disks are linked through situational
locations

4 - local links by repetition of the same selected disk:  in 14-15

5 - goals :
generic and general goal occurred in 3 'I can take again':  to free a peg (=
to free the peg that is able to be free). When the goal is achieved current
action is expanded to every automatic posible action such as  to put a disk
as big as possible on the free peg.
- in 4 : to free peg b; expanded after by move of disk 3 in 5
- in 6 : to free peg a ; expanded with situational location on disk 7-10,
and move of disk 3 in 11; expanded with situational location on peg c in
11-12, move of disk 2 in 13, and situational location on peg b in 13-14
- in 15:  to free peg b;  achieved in 15 and 16. The choice of the peg where
to put the disk was made with expansion by regrouping 14-15 (two successive
moves of the same disk). And then with expansion by move of a disk on the
free peg
- in 18:  to free peg b;  expanded with move of  disk 4 on the free peg;
expanded by move of another disk on peg a, but the only disk which can be
moved is the little one.  
- in 21:  to free peg c;  it starts with move of disk 2; then it was not
possible, it is an impass, nethertheless it was extended with situational
location on disk 2.   
- in 23:  to free peg c even if there is already another peg free.
