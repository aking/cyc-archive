OK, I 'm afraid I will stop reading at this point. Go ahead and 
partition all you want, and I will happily ignore these partitions 
and get on with my life without ever thinking about them again.

I was asking John Sowa to give us some clarification about what the 
'central things' ARE. (Remember "ontology" is supposed to be about 
what things are?) By clarification I didn't mean being told that they 
were 'nexuses' or could be called 'Thirdness'. Thats about as much 
help as being told that they are the footsteps on the road to true 
Nirvana, or manifestations of the singular otherness of the Eikarion, 
or whatever.

Imagine you are trying to explain this stuff to someone who isnt 
impressed by long words and has his feet planted firmly in the here 
and now; someone like an Irish carpenter, say. Then I might 
understand what you are talking about.
