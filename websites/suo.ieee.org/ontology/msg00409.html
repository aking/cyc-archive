John,
   It appears that you have a different understanding of what a "base 
document" is supposed to be than Jim did when he put this up for a 
vote.  Maybe he or possibly Frank Farance could clarify further.  My 
understanding is that it's just a concrete starting point for further 
work.  Your request to identify "trouble spots" and fix them sounds 
consistent to me with the process that results from agreement on a base 
document.

Adam
