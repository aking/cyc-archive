Jim,

Could you please elaborate on the rationale / motivation for this proposed
rule -- why should voting to Abstain allow one to keep a voting privilege
while not voting could cause a loss of voting privilege? -- isn't not voting
effectively the same as voting to Abstain?

Also, suppose two votes happen in the space of a few weeks. People who are
traveling might miss both votes, and find that they have lost voting
privileges. So it seems that this proposed rule could only be workable if
there were some other provisos regarding the frequency and duration of
votes...

Please advise,

Phil Jackson
