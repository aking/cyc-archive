	Thanks for the IFF link, it does look relevant and interesting.  Situation
theory and situation semantics come out of the same movement.  I think of
situation semantics as being closer to linguistics and NLP, while situation
theory is somewhat broader, addressing logic, mathematical modeling, and
occasionally even social theory.  But even making a clear distinction is
probably splitting hairs.
