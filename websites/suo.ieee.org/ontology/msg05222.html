Hello,

The IEEE P1600.1 "Standard Upper Ontology" (SUO) Working Group
maintains four separate archived discussion lists at this time:
 
General information about the working group objectives, along with
instructions for subscribing to any of the lists, may be found at
the group home page by following this link:
 
http://suo.ieee.org
 
What I always do to subscribe, or else to unsubscribe --
we do not have a "holiday", "no mail", or "vacation"
setting, so it is necessary to "on" and "off" a lot --
is just to send the following sorts of commands to:
 
<majordomo@ieee.org>
 
For example,
some subset of the following,
according to your wish at the time:
 
subscribe standard-upper-ontology
subscribe ontology
subscribe suo-kif
subscribe suo-ce
end
 
unsubscribe standard-upper-ontology
unsubscribe ontology
unsubscribe suo-kif
unsubscribe suo-ce
end
 
This should do it under normal circumstances.
 
Links to the Archives of the various lists are given here:
 
SUO (Old):

http://suo.ieee.org/email
http://suo.ieee.org/email/threads.html

Each of these is one big page, but they are kind of nice in that
you can search the whole of each page with a single find-in-page.

SUO (New):

http://suo.ieee.org/email/mail1.html
http://suo.ieee.org/email/thrd1.html

ONT >>>--->>> http://suo.ieee.org/ontology
CEL >>>--->>> http://suo.ieee.org/suo-ce
KIF >>>--->>> http://suo.ieee.org/suo-kif

[Note:  Not all of these last 3 are still funct.]

Please be sure to note that the address for subscribing and unsubscribing is:

<majordomo@ieee.org>

To subscribe or to unsubscribe, you send to the
above "majordomo" address a command message like
one of the following, according to your wish:

subscribe ontology
end

or

unsubscribe standard-upper-ontology
end

In order to distribute a message, however,
you send it to a different address, namely
one of the following, as the case may be:

SUO.  standard-upper-ontology@ieee.org
ONT.  ontology@ieee.org
CEL.  suo-ce@ieee.org
KIF.  suo-kif@ieee.org

If you have trouble with any of this, you may contact the Chair:
 
Jim Schoening <jim.s3@juno.com>
