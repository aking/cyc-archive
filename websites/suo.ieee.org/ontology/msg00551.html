> Hi All,

> 	I am considering making three major changes to the version of the
> merged ontology document that I sent out on January 12th, and I would like
> to solicit comments.  The proposed changes are as follows.
> 
> 1.  Simplify the tip of the ontology so that it has the following structure.

My concern about simplifying this is that you have already gone through
one phase of simplification, and this is the second one.
In neither phase did you have anything other than the terms themselves
in the ontology to define them, and so I couldn't figure out what 
was 'lost' in the previous simplification. 
I would much prefer a definition phase as the next one for the working
paper rather than another deletion phase.

> 
> (subclass-of Individual Entity)
> (subclass-of Relation Entity)
> (subclass-of Physical Entity)
> (subclass-of Abstract Entity)
> (subclass-of Continuant Entity)
> (subclass-of Occurrent Entity)
> 
You deleted Mediating. Why? I only saw one axiom for Mediating Entity:
(=> (and (instance-of ?m Mediating) (has ?m ?x) (has ?m ?y))
    (nec (or (has ?x ?y) (has ?y ?x))) )

did you delete Mediating because this includes nec ?
Or because Mediating is an example of Thirdness?
Or because no one has helped define it better in FOL terms?
Or some other reason? 

In fact in reviewing, I see you deleted Independent and Relative both.
So you want to delete this entire triad, not just one part of it...
Is that because Triads are controversial, or just hard to explain?
