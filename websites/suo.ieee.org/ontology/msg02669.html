Just by way of an explanation, though I really hate
to dispell the mystery of it all by detensing -- or
is it detenting? -- that willing suspension of your
disbelief too early, one of the prospects that I am
working toward on that thread about "Determination"
is the place in Peirce's history where he begins to
resolve the tension between Extension and Intension
by way of his notion of what he named "Information".
From that prospective perspective, the whole notion
of egging the logical dimensions of ex-&-in-tension
into a debate of the form "Ex" Versus "In", is just
another instance of the brands of fruitless battles
that dichotomous and dyadic thinking appears almost
determined constantly and continually to lead us to.
But it may take a while to get this look-out set up.

Jon Awbrey
