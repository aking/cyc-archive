Matthew,
   I think Pat's point is a bit simpler - that some reasonable choices in 
ontology are incompatible, so we'll never get a useful single ontology.  My 
point is the opposite - we can make some reasonable choices and get a 
coherent standard.  I take Cyc as an existence proof that reasonable 
choices can be made and the result is a product of significant value.

Adam
