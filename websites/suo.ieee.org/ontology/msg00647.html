> > > > > Here is a rough trial baloon for an agreement to 
> distinguish the
> > > > > referents for  SUO usage:
> > > > > 
> > > > > A relation of arity n, n>0,  is symbolized as predicate with 
> > > > > n arguments.
> > > > > The extension of a relation of arity 1 is the set of things 
> > > > > in the domain
> > > > > of quantification
> > > > > for which the predicate is true.  The  extension of a 
> > > > > relation of arity n>1
> > > > > is the
> > > > > subset of the nfold cartesian product of the domain of 
> > > > > quantification for
> > > > > which
> > > > > the predicate is true.
> > > > > 
> > > > > A relationship is an instance of a relation.    E.g.,  
> > > married(X,Y)
> > > > > describes a relation.
> > > > > married(Bill, Hillary) describes a relationship.
