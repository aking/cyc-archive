Hi All,

	David Whitten pointed out to me that the version of the merged
ontology loaded into the browser that we've made available is more extensive
than the last version which was submitted to the group.  I think that the
additions I have made are fairly uncontroversial, but, just so you have a
clear view of our work, we've created a publicly accessible mirror of the
version history of the merged ontology.  You can access this history
directly by clicking on
http://ontology.teknowledge.com/cgi-bin/cvsweb.cgi/SUO/.  I've also changed
the "source files" link on the general browser page
(http://ontology.teknowledge.com/rsigma/) so that it now points to the
version history site.  Note that this site contains the version history of
the sources of the merged ontology as well as the merged ontology itself,
which is called Merge.txt.  If you click on "Merge.txt", you will see links
to all of the versions of the merged ontology and a brief text description
of each version.

-Ian
