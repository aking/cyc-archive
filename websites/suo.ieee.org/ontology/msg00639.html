Jon,
   Parentheses need to balanced in KIF just as in Lisp.  If they weren't 
balanced in some axioms I sent out, that's just a typo on my part.  I think 
the axiom below should be

(forall (?t1 ?t2)
   (<=>
     (beforeEq ?t1 ?t2)
     (and
       (timepoint ?t1)
       (timepoint ?t2)
       (or
         (before ?t1 ?t2)
         (= ?t1 ?t2)))))

Adam
