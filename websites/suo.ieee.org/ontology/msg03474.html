Dear Jon,

By discussion document I mean anything that contributes towards the
development of a standard, but is not standards words. These might
be:

 - statement of requirements
 - standing documents for internal procedures
 - documents providing background theory/approach taken/proposed
   for the development of a standard.

If you want an example of what happens elsewhere, take a look at
the document log for ISO TC184/SC4/WG10. You can find this at:
http://www.pdtsolutions.co.uk/standard/wg10/index.html

I suspect you will find that a relatively small number of the
documents are actually standards wording.
