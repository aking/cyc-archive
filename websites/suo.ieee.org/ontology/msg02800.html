Adam,
        I have not try. It would be fine, I am sure that it would help me
for doing a more complete formalization of the solving process. But I don't
know how to do, and I am completely alone to do it. 
I guess at the SUO ontology, there are some terms such as 'on', 'then',
'so', which could be interesting. But I have not find verbs as take or put. 
Furthermore I feel as if it would need some more information about the
position of formal contextual components (in my jargon I call them
'invariant contextual parameters'). I did some work about that, it would be
more rigorous if this work was elaborated in the context of an ontology.       
        Another problem : my protocols are french ones. I don't know any
French ontology. But I can work on english protocols. I can take again the
Anzai and Simon protocol: its construction of rules, automatizations, and
the chunking of goals is very nice in this protocol.
I can work also on another kind of protocols. I already do it. But I think
it is better to concentrate efforts on ToH in a first time. 
        Sincerely yours
        Josiane
