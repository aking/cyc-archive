Matthew,

	Actually, instances of the 'nth-domain' predicate (e.g. (nth-domain
exists-at 2 TimePoint)) are well-formed SUO-KIF according to the document
posted at http://ltsc.ieee.org/suo/suo-kif.html.  Some of the relevant rules
of the BNF grammar are as follows:

relsent ::= (relword term+)
relword ::= initialchar wordchar*
term ::= variable | word | string | funterm | number

-Ian
