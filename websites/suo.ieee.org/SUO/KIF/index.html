SUO-KIF

IEEE Standard Upper Ontology (SUO) Study Group Knowledge Interchange Format

 

This standard will specify the syntax of a general-purpose knowledge representation language. The primary purpose of this effort is to support the SUO project. In addition, this effort should result in a standard that completes the standardization process begun in work on the Knowledge Interchange Format.
