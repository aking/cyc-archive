LarKC staff is giving Semantic Web lecture tour in China

Posted December 10th, 2008

Zhisheng Huang from the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam is giving a number of talks during a tour of various Chinese universities:

  • December 5, 2008, Towards Web3.0: How far will we go?, Southeast University, Nanjing, China.
  • December 6, 2008, Knowledge, Logics, and the Web: on the logical foundation of the Semantic Web, Southeast University, Nanjing, China
  • December 8, 2008, 14pm – 16pm, Towards Web3.0: How far will we go?, Jiangsu University of Science and Technology, Zhenjiang, China
  • December 8, 2009, 18:30 – 20:30, Ontology Reasoning and Management, Southeast University, Nanjing, Nanjing, China.
  • December 10, 2008, Towards Web 3.0: How far will we go?, Zhejiang University, Hangzhou, China;
  • December 23, 2008, Semantic Technology for Food Safety, The DoingCom Company, Xiamen, China.
