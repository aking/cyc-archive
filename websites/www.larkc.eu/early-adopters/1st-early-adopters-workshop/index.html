1st Early Adopters Tutorial

The first Early Adopters tutorial will be held in conjunction with ESWC 2009 in Crete on 1st of June. This will be the first opportunity for the research community to get their hands on LarKC results. In May we will release the first version of the LarKC platform that will be demonstrated and distributed to tutorial participants.

Participants will be introduced to the project by LarKC scientific coordinator Frank van Harmelen. The tutorial will provide hands on sessions with LarKC technology, introducing the participants to software delivered by the project and enabling them to create their own configurations, experiment with existing plug-ins and even build their own. They will also see the potential behind LarKC technology thanks to the demonstration based on project use cases

Program

  • Introduction to LarKC: general introduction, architecture, pipeline, existing plug-ins etc.
  • Play with LarKC: Tutorial participants have the opportunity to install LarKC and execute some queries.
  • Extend LarKC: Participants will be given instructions on how to re-configure a pipeline and how to modify plug-ins to achieve different behaviour.
  • Demonstration of the use cases
  • Coffee breaks and lunch
  • LarKC Reception

A more detailed program containing download links for slides can be found on the wiki.

Subscription to the tutorial is FREE. The maximum number of participants is 20. Participants will be selected in order of subscription. We will keep a stand by list, and in case of any withdrawal we will inform you.

This data is licensed from the Milan municipality and tutorial participants must register to use this data. The tutorial organizers will do this for you automatically unless you choose not to.
