Community

The goals of the LarKC project include the release of a number of open-source software components that together constitute a LarKC ‘instance’.

Ahead of the first (prototype) release of LarKC, we would like to start encouraging a community of users and developers to get involved.

For this reason, we have begun the process of making our development activities visible to the general public in the hope of improving support for users and developers and providing a means by which the community can provide feedback.
