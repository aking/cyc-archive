Proceedings of 1st International Workshop on Stream Reasoning (SR2009) published

Posted June 16th, 2009

The Workshop Proceedings have been published as CEUR Workshop Proceedings:

Emanuele Della Valle, Stefano Ceri, Dieter Fensel, Frank van Harmelen and Rudi Studer (Eds.): Proceedings of the 1st International Workshop on Stream Reasoning (SR2009), Heraklion, Crete, Greece May 31st, 2009.

CEUR Workshop Proceedings, Volume 466, is available at http://www.ceur-ws.org/Vol-466.
BibTeX of the Proceedings is also available at http://www.streamreasoning.org/bibtex/SR2009-proceeedings.bib.
