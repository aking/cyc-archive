Yi Zeng

Email: yzeng@emails.bjut.edu.cn Homepage: http://www.iwici.org/~yizeng

Yi Zeng is a Ph.D candidate at International WIC Institute (WICI), Beijing University of Technology. His main research interests are scientific literature analysis, knowledge retrieval (KR), etc. His research in LarKC focus on handling vague or incomplete queries and user-system interactions for interleaving reasoning.

Exchange Program

From 23rd May to 30th June 2009, Yi Zeng took the LarKC exchange program at Vrij University Amsterdam (VUA). During his visit, he was supervised by VUA team (mainly with Zhisheng Huang) on following topics:

  • User-centric Granular Search and Reasoning at Web Scale,
  • Formalization and logic foundations for granular reasoning,
  • Strategies design and evaluation of granular reasoning.

He also attended a course about "Ontology Engineering".

During this period of time, he worked with Zhisheng Huang on Deliverable 4.3.1 and Deliverable 4.7.1 prepared for M18 submission.
