Urban Computing in LarKC WP6

“Is public transportation where people are?” “Which place downtown is attracting more people right now?” “Where is the traffic moving?” Although the information is often available, there’s no software system capable of computing the answers - indeed, no system enables users even to issue such queries. With the help of LarKC technology, we are developing a Urban Computing showcase. It will demonstrate the possibility to answer those questions.
