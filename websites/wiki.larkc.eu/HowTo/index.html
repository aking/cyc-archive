Public pages

  • by default, the LarKC wiki will be open to the public
  • everybody can read anything, but only LarKC members can write/update/delete etc

  • to create a new page, just browse to it, e.g http://wiki.larkc.eu/MyNewPage

Private pages

  • if you want to make a page private (because it's confidential), you'll have to do that explicitly

    • Note: LarkcProject and below are no longer automatically closed

    • Note: settings are inherited: if you closed eg WP2, then WP2/SubPage will also be closed
  • to make a page private, include the following text at the top of the page:

    • #acl LarkcGroup:read,write,revert,delete,admin All:

    • the line states that only people in the LarkcGroup can read/write the page, others have no rights at all

Starting points

  • Communication: guide on which of our channels (website, wiki, blog, email) is where and for what

  • LarkcProject: root of project-related things

  • LarkcProject/WPs: pointers to all WP pages

  • LarkcProject/AdminForms: all things admin

  • RecentChanges: see where people are currently working

  • WikiSandBox: feel free to change this page and experiment with editing

  • FindPage: search or browse the database in various ways

  • HelpOnMoinWikiSyntax: quick access to wiki markup

  • SiteNavigation: get an overview over this site and what it contains

How to use this site

  • Edit any page by pressing Edit at the top or the bottom of the page

  • Create a link to another page with joined capitalized words (like WikiSandBox) or with [[words in brackets]]

  • Search for page titles or text within pages using the search box at the top of any page

  • See HelpForBeginners to get you going, HelpContents for all help pages.
