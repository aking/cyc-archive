LinkedLifeData demo service

?LinkedLifeData is platform to enable semantic data integration in early clinical and drug development process. Pathway and Interaction Knowledge Base is ontology hosted by ?LinkedLifeData (however the platform usability is not limited only to the selected data sources). The prototype application is available at:

URL: http://www.linkedlifedata.com

User & Password: (the service is public)

LifeSKIM demo service

LifeSKIM is commercial product of Ontotext. It automates the semantic annotations process of unstructured document. Our high-level plans are to run LifeSKIM application on top of ?LinkedLifeData and enrich the existing resources with information as result of information extraction. The prototype application is available at:

URL: http://lifeskim.semanticannotation.com

User & Password: (to obtain user and password please send a request to lifeskim@ontotext.com )

Disclaimer: Ontotext is not allowed to publicly redistributed Pubmed and SNOMED databases to anyone outside of the organization. By using the service you declare that either you are licensed to use the two databases in your organization or you are part of Ontotext. Use at your at your risk (e.g., please do not disclose the link to any public lists)!
