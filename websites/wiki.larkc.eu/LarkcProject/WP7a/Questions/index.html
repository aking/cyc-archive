This page outlines a list of questions, relation between concept types and concepts with their sources.

Questions

(many of the questionsare taken from http://esw.w3.org/topic/HCLSIG/PharmaOntology/Roles)

Higher priority

Q34. Tell me everything we know about this target? [C1-17]

Q35.1 What types of cancer are most common in COPD patients?

  • Q35.2 Are these cancer types more prevalent in COPD patients than in the general population?

    Q35.3 Which of these cancer types are related to smoking?

Q9. What is the function of this target (gene/protein/phenotype)? [R8]

Q15. What targets are associated with this disease? [R7]

Q16. What experiments have been conducted on this target (gene/protein/phenotype)? [R14]

Q17. What active compounds or structures affect this target? [R7]

Q19. What are the current hot research topic areas for disease x? [R10[C10] + timelines]

Q20. What is the disease phenotype? [R10[C10]]

Q28. What experiments have been conducted on this target (gene/protein/phenotype)? [R10]- gene/protein/phenotype, [R10]- experiment/assay

Q29. What experiments have altered the same pathway as this gene? [R10]- pathway, [R10]- experiment/assay

Lower priority

Q2. Provide a list of drugs approved by the FDA, EMEA and/or Japan for disease x. [R3]

Q3. Of drugs from either the same or different company for the same indication, are they approved in the same target region of interest – US or Global? [R1, R2, R3]

Q5. What generic drugs exist for the same indication? [R2]

Q8. Tell me everything we know about a gene? [C1]

Q31. What are the alternative names for the disease/condition? [C10] disease ontology

Q32. What are the alternative names for the symptom? [C10] symptom ontology

Not yet prioritized for various reasons

Q21. Do proteomic assays of tumor material and serum samples identify patterns reflecting outcomes? [R15]

Q24. How do the pathways, receptors and the drug response genes from the 5 different schizophrenia drugs compare? [R16, R7, R2]

Q27. Find all samples which are grade III colorectal cancer. For these sample, retrieve the expression, mutation and aCGH data [GEO]
