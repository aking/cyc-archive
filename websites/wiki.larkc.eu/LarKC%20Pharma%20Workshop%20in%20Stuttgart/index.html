LarKC Pharma workshop

The workshop was held on 19th and 20th April 2011 at High Performance Computing Center Stuttgart (HLRS), Germany, http://www.hlrs.de/

Objectives

The pharma workshop was based on previous LarKC tutorials and customized for drug development/discovery participants. The workshop gave participants the basic skills required to install and run their own experiments on the LarKC platform. Also some basics around how to develop your own plug-ins for the LarKC platform were shared.
