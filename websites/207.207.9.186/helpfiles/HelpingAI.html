Helping Artificial Intelligence

  • FACTory is much more than just a game. While you're playing, you're teaching an artificial intelligence called Cyc about the world. The game teaches Cyc the kinds of things that people know but computers often don't — in short, common sense. If you've ever wished your computer software would just show a little bit of common sense for once, FACTory may be a way to help get there.

  • Cyc is an AI system that has been under development since 1985. It knows enough now to come up with its own questions and theories about the world, and even to look for information on the web, and it has a lot of questions!

  • Needless to say, we also want FACTory to be an enjoyable game. Ideas? Questions? Let us know by making a suggestion.

    And don't forget to have fun playing!
