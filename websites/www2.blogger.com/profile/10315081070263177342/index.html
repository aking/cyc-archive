About Me

I am Principal Consultant at Objective Mastery, LLC and President of The Cyc Foundation, an independent, non-profit spinoff of Cycorp, Inc. The Cyc Foundation mission is to bring about a world in which every single person is given free access to software that reasons with the sum of all human knowledge. A primary focus of The Cyc Foundation will be to enlist the help of people from all walks of life in the task of teaching computers about the real world. I have been working for over 20 years in information technology. I started the OpenCyc and ResearchCyc projects at Cycorp in the late 1990s. Before that, I was Executive Director of IT for a $90M direct marketing retailer. OpenCyc, is the world's largest structured vocabulary of general purpose terminology, and it has been downloaded over 60,000 times. ResearchCyc is Full Cyc, but with a research-only license. ResearchCyc is being used by over 100 organizations in a dozen countries. I'm currently pursuing an MBA in Market Strategy from Regis University in Denver. I live with my wife and daughter in Austin, Texas.
