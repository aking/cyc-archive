Some Advanced Settings

The advanced inference settings are for users who are quite familiar with the CYC® System, especially the inference engine and its capabilities. Most users will not need to change the advanced settings from their defaults.

There are several settings listed on the Advanced Inference Settings page. Their descriptions are listed on that page, and will not be reproduced here.

Changing the Settings

To change any of the settings, select the desired radio button, then press the [Update Parameters] button. Doing so will make all changes at once, so be sure that each of the settings is as desired before updating the parameters.

The [Current Values] button will revert all radio buttons to the initial state they were in when this page was displayed.
