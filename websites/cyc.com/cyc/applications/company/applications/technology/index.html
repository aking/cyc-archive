the Cyc knowledge server

The Cyc Knowledge Server is a very large, multi-contextual knowledge base and inference engine developed by Cycorp. Cycorp's goal is to break the "software brittleness bottleneck" once and for all by constructing a foundation of basic "common sense" knowledge--a semantic substratum of terms, rules, and relations--that will enable a variety of knowledge-intensive products and services. Cyc is intended to provide a "deep" layer of understanding that can be used by other programs to make them more flexible. The Cyc technology includes the following components:
  • The Cyc Knowledge Base
  • The Cyc Inference Engine
  • The CycL Representation Language
  • The Natural Language Processing Subsystem
  • Cyc Semantic Integration Bus
  • Cyc Developer Toolsets
