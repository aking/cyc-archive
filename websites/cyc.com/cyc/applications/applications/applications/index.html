Cyc Information Products

The vast breadth of Cyc knowledge is now available to you as a series of domain-focused structured information products. Select from a library of pre-defined, interoperable domain taxonomies or let us provide custom-developed taxonomies or ontologies to meet your specific needs.

CycSecure: Information Network Risk Analysis

In addition to providing information products and an unparalleled knowledge-based development environment, we also offer a growing range of Cyc-based applications. One such application is CycSecure, a security risk analysis and network intrusion prevention tool. CycSecure leverages the power and richness of the Cyc Knowledge Base and reasoning capabiliteis to provide the network security professional with multi-leveled analyses of an organization's network vulnerabilities.

Cyc Analytic Environment (CAE)

The CAE is a database of structured knowldge about terrorists, terrorist organiations, and terrorist activities. More than that, it offers natural language querying and explanation capabilities to support analysis activities. Read an overview of the CAE or contact us to learn how this same technology can support other analytic enviornments (such as market analysis, financial analysis, and more)!
